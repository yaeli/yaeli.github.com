About Yaël

Hi, I'm Yaël, and I'm really passionate about creating simple, digital experiences that make people’s lives better.

I thrive in collaborative environments that value user-centred design, where I can work closely with other disciplines to make something really kickass. I love listening to how people make sense of their world, understanding what makes them tick, and solving problems for them.

I’ve previously worked with startups like JustGiving, Mind Candy and Moo, as well as consulted for agencies and big businesses like Cancer Research, Splendid and Story Worldwide.

I’m currently looking for short-term contract roles where I can craft delightful experiences and build, grow & enhance your ability to attract & engage customers!

UX Skills

Information Architecture, Sketching, Wireframing (Omnigraffle), Prototyping (Axure, HTML/CSS), Scenarios, User Flows, User Research, Usability Testing, Usability Reviews, Interaction Design, Copywriting.
