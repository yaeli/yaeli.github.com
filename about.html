About Bad Robot


Our mission is simple:

Be a Bad Robot!

A Bad Robot:

  • Always asks why.
  • Always questions the rules.
  • Uses initiative and goes the extra mile.
  • Values creativity over process.
  • Empathises and listens to others

We strive to be Bad Robots in everything that we do.


Bad Robot Design was founded on a sunny day in June 2012 by Yaël Levey, and is based in London, UK.


Bad Robot Design specialises in short-term user experience contract roles where we can craft delightful experiences and build, grow & enhance your ability to attract & engage customers.

About Yaël Levey



Hi, I'm Yaël, and I'm a user experience designer and founder of Bad Robot Design.

I'm really passionate about creating simple, digital experiences that make people’s lives better.

I thrive in collaborative environments that value user-centred design, where I can work closely with other disciplines to make something really kickass.

I love listening to how people make sense of their world, understanding what makes them tick, and solving their problems.

I’ve previously worked with startups like JustGiving, Mind Candy and Moo, as well as consulted for agencies and big businesses like Cancer Research, Splendid, AnalogFolk and Story Worldwide.
