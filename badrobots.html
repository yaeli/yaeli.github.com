Meet the Bad Robots

We specialise in short-term user experience contracts, where we can craft delightful experiences and build, grow & enhance your ability to attract & engage customers.


Yaël Levey

Director


Yael [Yah-Elle] is a user experience designer passionate about creating simple, digital experiences that make people’s lives better.

She loves listening to how people make sense of their world and understanding what makes them tick.

Yael's approach really reflects the spirit of a Bad Robot:

  • Always ask why.
  • Always question the rules.
  • Use initiative and go the extra mile.
  • Value creativity over process.
  • Empathise and listen to others

Skills:

Information Architecture | Sketching | Wireframing (Omnigraffle) | Prototyping (Axure, HTML/CSS) | Scenarios | User Flows | User Research | Usability Testing | Usability Reviews | Interaction Design | Copywriting

Yael has previously worked with startups like JustGiving, Mind Candy and Moo, as well as agencies and businesses like Cancer Research, Splendid, and AnalogFolk.
