Meet the Bad Robots

We specialise in user experience contracts, where we can craft delightful experiences and build, grow & enhance your ability to attract & engage your customers & prospects.


Yaël Levey

Director


Yael [Yah-Elle] is a senior user experience designer passionate about creating simple, digital experiences that make people’s lives better.

She loves listening to how people make sense of their world, understanding what makes them tick, and figuring out how to infuse the world of pixels with this user-centred knowledge.

Yael's Bad Robot ethos are:

  • Always ask why
  • Always question the rules
  • Use initiative and go the extra mile
  • Value creativity over process
  • Empathise and listen to others

Process:

  • Understand the problem
  • Understand the people who have the problem
  • Understand the constraints around the problem
  • Build out how you will know when your solution is successful
  • Work collaboratively with a multi-disciplinary team to come up with lo-fi solutions to the problem
  • Test continuously with users
  • Increase fidelity and depth of solution/s depending on project requirements
  • Help implement solution build by providing appropriate guidance and work collaboratively to make decisions
  • Test solution with users and iterate based on feedback
  • Make sure you can measure performance to see what you've achieved

Skills:

Facilitation | Information Architecture | Sketching | Wireframing (Omnigraffle) | Prototyping (Axure, HTML/CSS) | Scenarios | User Flows | User Research | Usability Testing | Usability Reviews | Interaction Design | Copywriting | Workshopping

Previously:

Yael has had over 5 years experience working with some of the most exciting startups and digital agencies in London.

She has worked with startups including JustGiving, Mind Candy and Moo, as well as agencies and businesses like Cancer Research, Splendid, and AnalogFolk.
